Despite their tendency to obscure simple concepts in a mess of natural language and quantified variables, pointwise definitions are abundant in the literature.  It doesn't have to be this way.  I have written a note
([found here](/assets/2021/01/27/upsets-and-downsets/Up_sets_and_down_sets.pdf)) showing how Oliveira's Pointfree Transform can be used to manipulate a pointwise definition from a well-known textbook into a particularly concise form.
